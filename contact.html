Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do you ensure the accuracy of medical information?

All our articles are reviewed by licensed medical professionals and are based on peer-reviewed research. We cite all sources and clearly indicate when research is preliminary or ongoing.

Can I submit research for coverage?

Yes! We welcome submissions from researchers and institutions. Please contact our editorial team with details about your research, including publication status and significance.

Do you offer collaboration opportunities?

We partner with research institutions, medical schools, and healthcare organizations to create educational content. Contact our partnerships team to discuss collaboration opportunities.

How often do you publish new content?

We publish new articles weekly, with breaking news and significant discoveries covered as they emerge. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.
